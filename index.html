The Village Daily

If there is no villain, there is no hero. That holds true for a story that unfolded late last night in the outskirts of Vynar. A knight named Sir Chuck found himself in a pickle after beheading a mighty dragon. He fought the beast for hours in a heroic attempt to save a princess in distress. But things took a turn for the worst when daylight broke, revealing the princess to be none other than Old Farmer Dinkleton--and the dragon corpse--his prized steer!
